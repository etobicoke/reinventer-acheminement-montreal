Three things that lose the submission at the last minute
  • A private URL. If the jury can't open it, it's worse than no link — test in incognito.
  • No video, or a video over 10 MB. It's an eligibility gate. Upload it before anything else.
  • A raw count that "looks wrong." It isn't — counts drift up, rates hold. But quote the rounded forms so nothing reads as a mismatch when the jury re-runs.
